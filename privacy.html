Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register for Smart X Academy, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, grade level, and phone number. When you use our feedback and comment systems, we collect the reviews you submit along with your display name.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our educational platform
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services and courses
  • Understand and analyze how you use our platform
  • Communicate with you for customer service and academic updates
  • Prevent fraud and ensure a safe community

3. Data Storage & Security

Your academic records, test scores, and registration data are stored securely. We implement reasonable security measures, including end-to-end encryption for critical data streams, to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, destruction, or alteration.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our academic partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses "cookies" and local storage to enhance the user experience (for instance, to keep you logged into the Teacher Feedback portal). You may choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies, but note that some parts of the site may not function properly as a result.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We have the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the updated date at the bottom of this page. We encourage students and parents to frequently check this page for any changes.

Contacting Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the practices of this site, please contact us at privacy@smartx.edu.et.
